# Visual Design Analysis of Power World Gyms
Based on the screenshots captured from the homepage:

### 1. Color Scheme
- **Primary Background**: Dark theme, predominantly black (`#000000`) and very dark grey.
- **Accent Color**: Vibrant Red (`#D80000` or similar) used for highlights, key buttons, icons, and active navigation states.
- **Text Color**: White (`#FFFFFF`) for primary headings and readability against the dark background. Light grey for body copy/secondary text.

### 2. Typography
- **Headings**: Strong, bold, uppercase sans-serif font (e.g., Impact, Montserrat, or similar heavy weight). Gives a powerful, energetic, and aggressive fitness vibe.
- **Body Text**: Clean, highly readable sans-serif font (e.g., Roboto, Open Sans).
- **Navigation**: Clean, uppercase sans-serif text, medium weight, turning red on hover.

### 3. Layout Patterns
- **Hero Section**: Full-screen or large background image with high-contrast text overlay, a bold catchphrase, and a primary CTA (Call to Action) button.
- **Content Sections**:
  - Grid layouts for displaying gym features, facilities, and programs (e.g., Cardio, Strength, Zumba).
  - High-quality, dark-filtered fitness photography as background or section images.
  - Alternating layout blocks (text left, image right, and vice versa) or grid cards.
- **Footer**: Standard dark footer with quick links, contact info, social links, and branch locations.

### 4. Button Styles
- **Primary CTA Buttons**: Solid red rectangular buttons with white or black text, sharp or slightly rounded corners. Often features a hover effect (e.g., color shift or glow).
- **Secondary Buttons**: Outline/ghost buttons (transparent background with red or white border).

### 5. Overall Aesthetic
- **Theme**: Dark, modern, energetic, and motivational.
- **Vibe**: Professional, intense, and focused, aligning with a high-performance fitness brand. High contrast is used to guide the user's eye to important details like joining or finding a branch.
